My Journal

My college life is not exactly all ups, there are also many downs. As life should be, not everything will be in the way that I like. This is the path that I choose and I will continue to walk through the end of this path. Playing games is one of my hobby and it always fascinates me whenever I play one. "How do these character moves?", "Look at this scene, it looks beautiful", "How do they make this?" These are some of the question I used to say when I was a kid playing a video game. And now that I have experience making one myself for a project in one of our subject. I'll say that it is pretty interesting. From the creation of characters, learning to making them move, where they will move, what action will they make when they hit an item and everything else. Its still feels like magic to me, that me and my groupmates made a game. Albeit a simple one, we still made a game. I want to continue in this path and see where would I be in the future.
